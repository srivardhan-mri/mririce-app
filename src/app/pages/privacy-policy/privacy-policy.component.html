2. Information We May Collect

We may collect various types of information in connection with the services we provide.

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, phone number, email address, delivery/shipping address, and business name/contact person (for wholesale or B2B clients). This is typically collected when you make an inquiry, place an order, or request a quote.
  • Business Information (for B2B clients): Business name, GSTIN, billing address, and other details necessary for processing wholesale orders and maintaining business accounts.
  • Order Information: Details of the rice varieties you are interested in or have ordered, quantities, packaging preferences, and order history.
  • Communication Data: Records of your communications with us, including emails, messages sent through our contact forms, or notes from phone calls regarding inquiries, orders, or support.
  • Derivative Data (Website Usage Information): Information our servers may automatically log when you access the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, and the pages you view. This is generally used for site maintenance and analytics to improve user experience.

3. How We May Use Your Information

Having accurate information permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the Site to:

  • Process and manage your inquiries, orders for rice, and requests for quotes.
  • Arrange for the shipping and delivery of your rice orders.
  • Manage your account, including B2B client accounts.
  • Communicate with you regarding your orders, inquiries, account status, and to provide customer support.
  • Send you information about our rice varieties, new harvests, special offers, or other updates, but only if you have opted-in to receive such communications. You will always have the option to unsubscribe.
  • Improve our website, product offerings, and customer service based on your feedback and general usage patterns.
  • Ensure the security of our Site and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations in India.
  • Maintain internal records for administrative and business purposes.

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information we have collected about you in certain situations. Your information may be disclosed as follows:

  • By Law or to Protect Rights: If we believe the release of information about you is necessary to respond to legal process, to investigate or remedy potential violations of our policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of others, we may share your information as permitted or required by any applicable Indian law, rule, or regulation.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Site may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to help customize the Site, analyze usage, and improve your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve our Site and your experience, see which areas and features of our Site are popular, and count visits.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our Site.
